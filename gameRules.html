Royal Blackjack

Game Rules

  1. Objective:
    The objective of the game is to accumulate points by forming a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it, while also outscoring the dealer. The first player to reach 10 points wins the game.

  2. Game Setup:
    • The game is played between a single player and the dealer.
    • Each player starts with a score of zero.
    • A standard 52-card deck is used.


  3. Gameplay:
    • The player initiates the game by pressing the "Deal" button.
    • Upon dealing, the player receives a pair of cards facing up, and the dealer receives two cards facing down.
    • The player has the option to either "Hit" (receive an additional card) or "Stand" (keep the current cards) to improve their hand's value.
    • If the player's hand value exceeds 21, they go "Bust," and the round is automatically won by the dealer.
    • If the player's initial hand value equals 21, they achieve "Blackjack" and win the round automatically.
    • Once the player stands, the dealer reveals their facedown card.
    • The dealer must draw additional cards ("Hit") until their hand value is 17 or higher.
    • If the dealer's hand exceeds 21, they go "Bust," and the player wins the round.
    • The round concludes when both the player and dealer stand.
    • The winner of the round is determined by comparing the hand values of the player and the dealer. The closest to 21 without exceeding it wins the round.
    • The winner of each round earns one point.
